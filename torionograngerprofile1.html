Summary of a Heck of a Guy

Toriono Granger is a native Chicagoan and a graduate of the University of Illinois at Urbana-Chanpaign wuth a bachelor of science degree in Mechanical Engineering. After 15 years as an engineer in various industries, Toriono enrolled at the Kellogg School of Management where he ultimately completed his MBA in 2013 majoring in Strategy, Marketing, and Entreprenuership and Innovation. Toriono is named on several patents in a range of industries from strollers, household cleaning products, ear protection, to the gaming industry.

Currently, Toriono is a manager at Radius Product Development, a product development and innovation consultancy, where he is a leader in the engineering and implementation service. Toriono resides on the southside of Chicago with his wife Nathalia and their two daughters. Toriono's interests include stain glass, real estate entreprenuership, economic development in the inner city, anything related to World History (particularly African history), and spending time with family.
